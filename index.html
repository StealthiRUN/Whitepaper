Real Estate Strategy

Rental property investments should follow a five-year buy-to-sell plan, accounting for ongoing expenses such as property taxes, home insurance, and utilities. When a property is sold, funds will be disbursed to the designated property's NFT collection. By purchasing homes outright, we eliminate mortgage-related costs, ensuring that our assets are held with less ongoing expense risk.

Units will be rented out and token holders can book accommodations in any of our owned properties using $RUN tokens. Holders will have priority to book a stay, before units are made available to the public. All rental incomes will be retained to cover fixed and variable expenses for the unit. $RUN tokens collected will be retained, burned, airdropped, or added to liquidity.
